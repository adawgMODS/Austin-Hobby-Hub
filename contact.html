PC Game Reviews

Featured PC Game Reviews

Check out my favorite game reviews below.

The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion Remastered

Oblivion Remastered takes a game that was already amazing and makes it feel brand new. I played the original when it first came out in 2006, so seeing the updated graphics and modernized menus is incredible.

Since it's been so long, it almost feels like I'm experiencing the game for the first time again, while still getting that nostalgic feeling reminds me of why I fell in love with the game love it in the first place.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is one of the most impressive games I've ever played because of its realism. The wildlife, environments, characters, and the countless ways you can approach different situations make the world feel alive.

Combined with an incredible story, beautiful graphics, a massive open world, and excellent optimization on PC at 1080p, it's easily one of my favorite games to get lost in.

Subnautica 2

I've spent countless hours surviving, exploring, and building in both the original Subnautica and Below Zero, so I was excited to jump into Subnautica 2. Even though it's still in Early Access and there's a lot more content to come, my first impression has been, "I'm playing an amazing game that isn't quite finished yet."

As the developers continue building on what they've started, I think it has the potential to be far better than the original.

My overall rating took a slight hit, but that's expected with any Early Access game. I have no doubt that in a year or two, the rating will be a 5/5.
